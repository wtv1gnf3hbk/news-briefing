Good morning. Here's the state of play:

The war with Iran is escalating fast, and it's dragging in the entire Gulf region. After the U.S. [bombed Iran's](https://www.nytimes.com/live/2026/03/14/world/iran-war-trump-oil-israel) key Kharg Island oil terminal yesterday, Iran's Revolutionary Guard is now calling American bases in the UAE "legitimate targets," according to [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/3/14/iran-war-live-pentagon-vows-to-ramp-up-us-military-campaign-against-iran). That threat became reality overnight when Iran's navy claimed strikes on U.S. forces in Abu Dhabi and Bahrain. The UAE's crucial Fujairah oil hub suspended operations after what Bloomberg calls a [drone attack](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-14/some-fujairah-oil-operations-suspended-after-drone-attack-fire), sending Brent crude above $103 a barrel for the first time in nearly four years.

The regional spillover is exactly what energy markets feared. Oil-dependent economies from Pakistan to India are scrambling for alternatives, with Iraq now [seeking routes](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/3/14/iran-war-live-pentagon-vows-to-ramp-up-us-military-campaign-against-iran?update=4397926) around the Strait of Hormuz for its exports, according to Al Jazeera. Meanwhile, the Pentagon is [deploying more](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq8gj9n12g8o) Marines and warships to the region, the BBC reports.

Back home, Trump and Secretary of State Rubio are framing this as their ["destroy and deal"](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/14/us/politics/rubio-trump-iran-war.html) strategy, banking on military pressure to force negotiations. But the approach is reshaping America's [alliance with Israel](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/13/us/politics/trumps-war-alliance-with-israel-is-reshaping-the-middle-east-but-it-carries-risks.html) and drawing pushback from Democrats like Tim Kaine, who's trying to [force a debate](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/14/us/politics/tim-kaine-iran-war-congress.html) on congressional war powers.
