The Pentagon confirmed six US soldiers have died in the conflict so far, [the BBC reports](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gq33ynl07o).

• AI companies made a White House pledge to [pay for power](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/04/technology/ai-energy-pledge-white-house-trump.html) as Trump announced new industry commitments to address energy concerns around data centers.
